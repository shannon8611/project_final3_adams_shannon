Welcome to Cat Nap Pet Sitting!

Pet Sitting Exclusively for Cats

Cat Nap Pet Sitting is a premier, in-home pet sitting service exclusively for cats. Our goal is to make your time away from home much easier by providing an alternative to the added stresses of boarding. We believe that cats do better within the comfort of their own home and that is why our service is designed to bring the pet care experience directly to you.
